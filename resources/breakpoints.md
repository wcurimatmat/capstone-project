# Media Web Breakpoints

**Extra small devices such as small phones**

1. 360px (minimum)
2. 639px (maximum)

**Small devices such as large phones**

1. 640px and up

**Medium Devices such as tablets**

1. 768px and up

**Large devices such as laptops**

1. 1024px and up

**Largest devices such as desktops**

1. 1280px and up
2. Can use 1440px for web content constraint
